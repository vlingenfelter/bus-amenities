Bus Amenities Tool

How to use this tool:

Step 1:

Choose the dataset that will be used for the ranking.

Step 2:

Use the sliders to weight different qualities to your liking. The weights you choose will change the criteria for which buses are selected for getting new amenities.

When you are satisfied with your selection, press SUBMIT

Step 3:

Review the output of your selections and rankings in the map viewer. It is at this point that you can click DOWNLOAD DATA to export the data from your selections as a .csv
